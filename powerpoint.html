This page will help you get started using Powerpoint to make adapted books

First, you need to open Office365 Powerpoint and log in with your Elim credentials.

PowerPoint books should include:
- A cover page with a picture and title (titles listed below).
- 8-10 pages
- At least 1 photo on each slide. No more than 3 photos per slide
- 1-3 sentences of text
- 2-3 YouTube video links on the last slide
(preview to make sure they are school appropriate first)
- Videos should be between 2-8 minutes long.
